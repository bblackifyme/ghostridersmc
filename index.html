About the club

We are a small non-profit organization established in 1997 to promote the sport of off-road riding, to rally to protect the interests of motorcyclists both on and off-road, and to support the local communities in which we ride.

We are charter members of the AMA and District 36, for the past 20 years our Annual Phantom Scramble has been included in the Series Championship for Northern California.
